We are a dual disabled U.S. Army veteran family learning anything and everything we can on how to homestead/farm. We bought our 3 acre property in June 2023.

We have added new members to our farm! We now have added 8 Rabbits, 3 nigerian dwarf Goats, 25 Chickens (Black Australorps, New hampshire Reds, Splash Americaunas, Bielefelders, French Blue Copper Marans) and started our own farm garden.!

This month we planted 17 rows of different veggies! We know groceries are starting to cost, but we have a plan of attack to feed our family of 5!
